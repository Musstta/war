# Scenario: pop-transfer-compat

> Two simultaneous population transfers with contrasting cultural compatibility. Pair A: Nicaragua sends 20 pop to Costa Rica (both Latin family, similar traits) — receiver culture similar to nicaragua → high compat → small populationTransferShock. Pair B: Belize (European family, individualist/progressive) sends 20 pop to Guatemala (Latin family, militaristic/collectivist) — different family (latin|european closeness=0.60) + divergent value traits → lower compat → larger populationTransferShock. Both transfers fire at T2 (transferAtTick=2). Expected: shock component present at T3 (SHOCK_DURATION=5 ticks, fired T2, gone T7+). Metric output confirms shock magnitude on belize/guatemala > shock on costa_rica/nicaragua, validating the v0.40 fix: populationTransferShockMagnitude is stored per-territory (instead of the discarded `void shockMagnitude` that used a uniform fixed shock).
